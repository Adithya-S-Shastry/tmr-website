Technical Specifications

OUR SUBSYSTEMS

Precision-engineered components driving off-road excellence through rigorous computational analysis and mechanical innovation.

Structures

The structures subsystem designs, analyzes, and fabricates the tubular chassis for durability, while also crafting basalt fiber and Kevlar bodywork to enhance strength and aesthetics.

Transmission

Our 4WD transmission delivers 700 Nm of torque, towing a 2-ton tractor in 15 seconds and accelerating 150 feet in 4.5 seconds. Optimized gear ratios and advanced materials ensure superior traction, stability, and efficiency.

Vehicle Dynamics

We design suspension, braking, and steering systems to optimize off-road handling, ensuring precise steering, driver comfort, and stability in demanding terrain. Our approach enhances responsiveness and durability for superior performance.

Electronics

This subsystem focuses on designing EV batteries, developing control systems, and refining powertrain components. It also involves building custom wiring harnesses that integrate various electronic modules, ensuring efficient energy management and vehicle performance.

Data Acquisition

This part involves collecting and analyzing real-time vehicle data to optimize efficiency, tuning, and overall performance. It supports decision-making by monitoring parameters like battery status, motor behavior, and environmental conditions, enabling precise diagnostics and system refinement.

Management

The Management team handles Finance, Logistics, Media & PR, Sponsorships, and Sourcing. Finance and Logistics manage transactions, Media & PR oversee branding, while Sponsorships and Sourcing secure funding and essential resources.

Research & Development

The research team focuses on filing patents and writing research papers. Working with other subsystems, they innovate new components and optimize existing components.
