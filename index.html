The Competition

DISCOVERING
E-BAJA

The e-Baja competition is a prestigious arena where engineering students design, build, and race electric-powered all-terrain vehicles. Organized by the Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE), this elite competition emphasizes sustainable engineering practices and cutting-edge powertrain technology.

Teams from premier institutions demonstrate their creativity and technical prowess by constructing efficient, durable off-road vehicles. The event provides critical, practical engineering experience while forging environmental consciousness among the next generation of innovators.
