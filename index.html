About Us

WE ARE DRIVEN BY A PASSION FOR DESIGNING, BUILDING, AND TESTING ALL-TERRAIN ELECTRIC VEHICLES FOR NATIONAL-LEVEL COMPETITIONS. OUR CONSISTENT SUCCESS IN WINNING PRESTIGIOUS AWARDS AND CASH PRIZES REFLECTS OUR UNWAVERING COMMITMENT TO EXCELLENCE, INNOVATION, AND THE FUTURE OF ELECTRIC MOBILITY. OUR TEAM SPECIALIZES IN ENGINEERING AND OPTIMIZING CRITICAL VEHICLE COMPONENTS AND ELECTRICAL SYSTEMS, CULMINATING IN A HIGH-PERFORMANCE, 9 KW SINGLE-SEATER DESIGNED TO RIVAL THE CAPABILITIES OF ITS COMBUSTION-ENGINE COUNTERPARTS.

Subsystems

Structures

The structures subsystem designs, analyzes, and fabricates the tubular chassis for durability, while also crafting basalt fiber and Kevlar bodywork to enhance strength and aesthetics.

Transmission

Our 4WD transmission delivers 700 Nm of torque, towing a 2-ton tractor in 15 seconds and accelerating 150 feet in 4.5 seconds. Optimized gear ratios and advanced materials ensure superior traction, stability, and efficiency.

Vehicle Dynamics

We design suspension, braking, and steering systems to optimize off-road handling, ensuring precise steering, driver comfort, and stability in demanding terrain.

Electronics

This subsystem focuses on designing EV batteries, developing control systems, and refining powertrain components. It also involves building custom wiring harnesses.

Data Acquisition

This part involves collecting and analyzing real-time vehicle data to optimize efficiency, tuning, and overall performance through targeted parameters tracking.

Research & Development

The research team focuses on filing patents and writing research papers. Working alongside other disciplines, they actively innovate or refine core custom systems.
